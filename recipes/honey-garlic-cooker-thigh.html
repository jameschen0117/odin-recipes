Cooker Thigh

Description

Honey-Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thigh
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Key Points

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Time: 90 mins
  • Total Time: 1 hrs 35 mins
  • Servings: 4

Steps

  1. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over chicken thighs.
  2. Cover and cook for 1 hours.
