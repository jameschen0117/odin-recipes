Bake Thigh

Description

Easy Baked Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken Thighs

Key Points

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Time: 30 mins
  • Total Time: 35 mins
  • Servings: 4

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken thighs in a baking dish; season both sides with garlic powder and onion flakes.
  3. Bake about half an hour until no longer pink at the bone and juices run clear.
  4. Insert thermometer into the thickest part of the thigh, near the bone.
  5. Read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), otherwise keeps baking.
